Getting Started

Welcome to the Community Event Portal! This guide will help you navigate and use all the features available to you.

Navigation

The main navigation bar at the top of the page allows you to quickly jump to different sections:

  • Home: Returns to the welcome banner and main content
  • Events: View all upcoming community events
  • Gallery: Browse images from past events
  • Register: Sign up for upcoming events
  • Feedback: Share your feedback about attended events
  • Video: Watch promotional videos
  • Location: Find events near your current location
  • Contact: Get in touch with us

Viewing Events

  • Events are displayed as cards showing the event name, date, location, and brief description
  • Use the category filters on the left sidebar to filter events by type
  • Click "All Events" to see all available events
  • Your preferred event category is saved automatically

Event Gallery

  • The gallery displays images from past community events
  • Images are arranged in a 3-column table layout
  • Double-click any image to enlarge it for a better view
  • Each image has a caption describing the event

Registering for Events

  • Fill in your full name, email, and phone number
  • Select an event from the dropdown menu
  • Specify the number of attendees (1-10)
  • Agree to the terms and conditions
  • Click "Register Now" to complete your registration
  • You'll receive a confirmation message after successful registration

Submitting Feedback

  • Share your experience from any event you've attended
  • Your phone number will be validated when you leave the phone field
  • Enter your feedback (up to 500 characters) - the character counter tracks your progress
  • Rate the event on a scale of 1-5
  • Click "Submit Feedback" to share your thoughts

Using Geolocation

  • Click the "Find Nearby Events" button in the Location section
  • Your browser will ask for permission to access your location
  • Grant permission to see your current latitude and longitude
  • This information helps you find events near your location

Promotional Video

  • Watch our promotional video about community events
  • Use the video controls to play, pause, and adjust volume
  • You'll see a "Video ready to play" message when the video is loaded

Saving Preferences

  • Your preferred event category is automatically saved to your browser
  • When you return to the site, your preference is restored
  • Click "Clear Preferences" to reset your saved preferences

Frequently Asked Questions

Scroll down on the main page to find an accordion section with answers to common questions about our events.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Check the Events section regularly for new community activities
